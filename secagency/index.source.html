About us

DJA Security Services Inc. (“The Agency”) started as a single proprietorship duly registered with the Department of Trade and Industry on 10 September 2000. However, only in August 2002 did it acquire its License to Operate (LTO) and in October 2002 did it commence commercial operation. In October 2006, keeping attuned with its growing business operations, the Agency changed its legal personality into a corporation.

Organized by lawyers Philip S. Dionisio and J. Tobias M. Javier to initially cater to the clients of their law firm, Dionisio Javier & Argamosa (DJA) Law Offices, the Agency grew in a relatively fast-paced manner as its clientele base soon expanded to non-clients of DJA Law Offices. In February 2003, the Agency became one of the accredited security providers of SM Supermalls.

Presently, the Agency has a good number of postings at SM Manila, SM Sucat, SM San Lazaro, SM Molino, SM Sta. Rosa, SM Pasig-Frontera Verde, Mezza I Residences, SM Mall of Asia, SM Savemore, and SM Hypermarket. It boasts of more than eight hundred (800) security guards in its roster of troops who are all stringently screened, properly trained and well-equipped. Aside from SM, it also provides security services to Samsung Electronic Phils. Corp., Lotus Central Mall, Alfamart (Cavite Area), China Banking Corporation, Banco de Oro and other clients ranging from restaurants/bars to residential enclaves to mining/quarrying companies within and outside Metro Manila.

The Agency holds office at No. 168 Sinciego Street, Brgy. 18, Pasay City.
