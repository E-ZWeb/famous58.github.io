About Me

A quick biographical about myself and how I relate to the topic

    I grew up in the state of New Mexico and have known some family members who oppose vaccination and that is what has sparked my interest in the topic. I've learned a lot through the research I've done for this compaign about anti-vaccination and vaccines themselves and just how successful vaccines have been in saving millions of lives. I've also lived for some years in 3rd world countries where I have seen the developing state of healthcare and the struggles of these nations in getting vaccine converage and controlling the spread of disease in their growing economies. It is my sincere intention with this campaign to spread the awareness of herd vaccination and Herd Immunity.
