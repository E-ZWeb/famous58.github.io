Berenghinya Planitia

Your destination, the Berenghinya Planitia, is a 3,900km wide formation of the mysterious venus arachnoids. When we arrive after a 2hour high speed flight from the station on venus, there will be ATVs designed to roam the rocky venutian surface. Explore the vast landscapes of the arachnoids where you can take photographs and have a family picnic on top of the ancient volcanic hills. In addition we will provide specialized containers for those who wish to bring back a geological sample of the arachnoid surface.
Not responsible for potential food poisoning caused by toxins in the venutian atmosphere.

Ancient Geological Wonders

Gigantic ancient volcanic formations on the surface of venus ranging from 50 to 230 km in diameter. Little is known about the origin of the arachnoids. Not even accurate color images exist of them. All we know is they are big and they kinda look like spiders. Be the first to see these enigmatic locations in person.
We cannot guarantee that you will indeed be the first to see these in person. Many geologists may have already surveyed them on expeditions to venus
